## Risk Assessment

### Technical Risks
- **Cold start problem**: New genomes need 10-20 interactions to optimize
  - Mitigation: Starter genomes, transfer learning

- **Extreme edge cases**: Rare scenarios may take longer to adapt
  - Mitigation: Human-in-the-loop approval, fallback prompts

### Business Risks
- **Market education**: Novel concept requires explanation
  - Mitigation: Clear documentation, video demos, case studies

- **Competition**: Large players (OpenAI, Anthropic) could replicate
  - Mitigation: First-mover advantage, build community, open ecosystem

### Adoption Risks
- **Developer skepticism**: "Is this better than manual prompting?"
  - Mitigation: Public benchmarks, open-source core, scientific validation
